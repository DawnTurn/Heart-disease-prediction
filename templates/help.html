Help and Information

How It Works

Input Your Data

Fill out a simple form with your health information such as age, cholesterol levels, blood pressure, and more.

Get Your Prediction

Our system analyzes your data and provides an accurate prediction of your heart disease risk.

Receive Recommendations

Based on your prediction, receive personalized recommendations on how to improve and maintain your heart health.

Understanding the Attributes

  • Age: The age of the individual in years.
  • Sex: The gender of the individual (1 = male, 0 = female).
  • CP (Chest Pain Type): The type of chest pain experienced:
    • 0: Typical angina
    • 1: Atypical angina
    • 2: Non-anginal pain
    • 3: Asymptomatic
  • Trestbps (Resting Blood Pressure): The resting blood pressure (in mm Hg).
  • Chol (Serum Cholesterol): The serum cholesterol level (in mg/dl).
  • FBS (Fasting Blood Sugar): Whether the fasting blood sugar is > 120 mg/dl (1 = true, 0 = false).
  • Restecg (Resting Electrocardiographic Results): The resting ECG results:
    • 0: Normal
    • 1: Having ST-T wave abnormality
    • 2: Showing probable or definite left ventricular hypertrophy
  • Thalach (Maximum Heart Rate Achieved): The maximum heart rate achieved during exercise.
  • Exang (Exercise Induced Angina): Whether exercise-induced angina occurs (1 = yes, 0 = no).
  • Oldpeak: ST depression induced by exercise relative to rest.
  • Slope: The slope of the peak exercise ST segment:
    • 0: Upsloping
    • 1: Flat
    • 2: Downsloping
  • CA (Number of Major Vessels): The number of major vessels (0-3) colored by fluoroscopy.
  • Thal: Thalassemia:
    • 0: Normal
    • 1: Fixed defect
    • 2: Reversible defect
